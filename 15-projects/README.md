# Projects

<p class="lead">Projects is where the repository connects multiple layers at once. These pages turn theory from foundations, delivery, runtime, cloud, and SRE into realistic stories that feel closer to actual work.</p>

## What This Section Helps You See

<div class="section-grid">
  <div class="panel-card">
    <div class="icon-chip ops">CASE</div>
    <h3>Systems in context</h3>
    <p>Project stories show how architecture, delivery, runtime, security, and observability interact under real constraints.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="panel-card">
    <div class="icon-chip devops">FLOW</div>

  </div>
  <div class="panel-card">

</div>

## End-to-End Story Flow

```mermaid
flowchart LR
  A[Problem or migration] --> B[Architecture choices]
  B --> C[Delivery path]
  C --> D[Runtime and operations]
  D --> E[Reliability lessons]
  E --> F[Platform improvements]
```

<p class="diagram-note">Projects are where the lower layers and higher-level platform decisions finally meet in one operational story.</p>
